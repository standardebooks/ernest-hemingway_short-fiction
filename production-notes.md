There are 5 missing stories, none of which are usually included in Hemingway's short story collections. Three are juvenilia: "The Judgment of Manitou," "A Matter of Color," and "Sepi Jingan" published in Hemingway's highschool magazine. The other two are "Christmas in Paris" and "A North of Italy Christmas" both published on December 22, 1923 in The Toronto Star Weekly. I was unable to find scans/transcriptions of any.
